Drama Club

Welcome to the Drama Club

The Drama Club is a vibrant community where storytelling, acting, and stagecraft come to life. Whether you're an aspiring actor, a passionate scriptwriter, or someone who enjoys the magic of theatre, this is the place for you. Our club offers opportunities to participate in plays, improve public speaking skills, and explore the art of performance.

Our Activities

  • Stage Performances - Participate in grand productions and showcase your talent.
  • Improvisation Workshops - Enhance your spontaneity and creativity.
  • Scriptwriting Sessions - Learn the art of writing compelling stories.
  • Backstage Management - Get hands-on experience with lights, sound, and set design.
  • Theatre Appreciation - Explore the history and impact of drama worldwide.
